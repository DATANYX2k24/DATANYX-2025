Moon Texture Setup Required

To see the moon effect, please download the following NASA textures:

Step 1: Download NASA Moon Textures

NASA CGI Moon Kit - Download Here

Step 2: Place Files

Rename and place these files in your public/assets/textures/ folder:

  • moon_color.jpg - Main moon surface texture
  • moon_normal.jpg - Surface detail/bump map
  • moon_disp.jpg - Height/displacement map (optional)

Step 3: Refresh

Reload the page to see the moon effect with textures!

NASA textures are free for educational and non-commercial use.
